France

Road to the World Cup

With their winning of the 2018 Fifa World Cup in Russia, France acquired their second World Cup in history. Their first was in 1998.

France World Cup History

Although France has only Won two World Cups they have been close several other times. They placed 4th in 1982, 3rd in 1958 and 1986, and 2nd in 2006 which they lost in penalties.

Soccer Beginnings

The French national team was formed in 1904.

Worldwide

Soccer Players from France don't just play in France they play thorughout Europe and the world.

World Cup Count

France joined Uruguay and ARgentina who both have 2 World Cup wins.

1998 Team

The first French team to win the World Cup was the 1998 team. They won all 7 of their games on the way to winning the World Cup.

In the group stage they beat South Africa and Denmark but in their win over Saudi Arabia one player from each team received a red card. In the round of 16 they beat Paraguay 1-0. In the Quarter-finals it came to a nail biter finish as France barely came out on top versus Italy, winning 3-4 on penalty kicks. In the semis one player from France got a red card but they still managed to win 2-1. In the finals they stromed to a 3-0 win over Brazil.

2018 Team

To go all the way and win the World Cup France had to go through seven countries. During group play they beat both Austrailia and Peru, and tied Denmark. In the round of 16 they beat out Argentina 4-3. They won 2-0 against Uruguay in the Quarter-finals. In the semis they won 1-o over Belgium. In the Finals they defeated Croatia 4-2.

Even though they won, the games they played were tough. They only won by one goal in 4 out of their 6 wins. Those games could have gone very differently but France still managed to fight their way through.
